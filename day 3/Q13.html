Should Plastic be Banned Essay for Students and Children

It has become very essential to ban plastic bags before they ruin our planet completely. Many countries around the globe have either put a ban on the plastic bag or Levi tax on it. However,
the problem hasn’t been solved completely because the implementation of these measures hasn’t been as successful.
