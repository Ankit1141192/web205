Reasons for the digital divide

  1. People living in some rural or remote areas often have poor or no access to reliable internet.
  2. Some individuals lack education or training to understand how to use technology effectively.
  3. Some people cannot afford devices like computers, smartphones, or internet services.
  4. Older people may find it harder to learn and adapt to new technologies.
  5. People with disabilities may not have access to technology that works well for them or meets their needs.
